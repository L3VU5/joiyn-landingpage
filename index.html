Features

Everything you need to connect locally

From discovering nearby events to chatting with participants before you arrive, Joiyn covers the full journey of making a real-world connection.

🗺️

Discover Events Nearby

Browse activities happening in your city, filtered by your interests and how far you're willing to go.

🎨

Pick Your Interests

Tell Joiyn what you love — hiking, coffee, board games, fitness — and get matched to events that fit your vibe.

➕

Create Your Own Event

Organise a small gathering in minutes. Set the time, place, and how many people you want to invite.

💬

Chat Before You Meet

Get to know participants beforehand so showing up feels comfortable, not awkward.

🔔

Stay in the Loop

Get notified when events are confirmed, cancelled, or when someone new joins the group.

⭐

Rate & Reconnect

After every event, leave quick feedback and send instant connections to people you'd love to meet again.

How it works

Three steps to your next real-life connection

No endless scrolling. No algorithms pushing content. Just genuine people who want to do the same things as you.

🙋

Build your profile

Create an account, choose your interests, and set your location. Takes less than two minutes.

🔍

Browse & join

Explore events in your city. Filter by activity type, date, or group size — then tap to join.

🤝

Show up & connect

Attend the event, meet the people, and build connections that last beyond the meetup.
