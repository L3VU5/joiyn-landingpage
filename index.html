Features

Everything you need to meet people locally

Browse events, chat with the group before you arrive, and keep in touch with people you'd want to see again.

Discover Events Nearby

Browse activities happening in your city, filtered by your interests and how far you're willing to go.

Pick Your Interests

Tell Joiyn what you love: hiking, coffee, board games, fitness. We'll match you to events that fit your vibe.

Create Your Own Event

Organise a small gathering in minutes. Set the time, place, and how many people you want to invite.

Chat Before You Meet

Stay in the Loop

Rate & Reconnect

How it works

Three steps to your next meetup

Pick your interests, find something happening nearby, and show up. That's pretty much it.

Build your profile

Create an account, choose your interests, and set your location. Takes less than two minutes.

Browse & join

Explore events in your city. Filter by activity type, date, or group size. Then tap to join.

Show up & connect

Attend the event, meet the people, and stay in touch with whoever you click with.
